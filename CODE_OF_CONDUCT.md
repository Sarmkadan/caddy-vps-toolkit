# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. We pledge that everyone participating in the caddy-vps-toolkit community and its projects will experience an environment free of harassment and discrimination.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment include:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members
- Helping other members learn and grow

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- The use of sexualized language or imagery and unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information, such as a physical or email address, without explicit permission
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting
- Discrimination based on race, gender, gender identity and expression, sexual orientation, disability, personal appearance, body size, caste, age, religion, or national origin

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Community leaders are responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards of acceptable behavior and will take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any behavior that they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.

Community leaders will:

- Remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, wiki edits, issues, and other contributions that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct
- Ban temporarily or permanently any contributor for behaviors that they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all community spaces, and also applies when an individual is officially representing the community in public spaces. Examples of representing our community include using an official e-mail address, posting via an official social media account, or acting as an appointed representative at an online or offline event.

## Reporting Violations

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the project maintainers. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

**How to Report:**

1. **GitHub**: Use GitHub's private vulnerability reporting at https://github.com/sarmkadan/caddy-vps-toolkit/security/advisories/new
2. **Email**: rutova2@gmail.com
3. **Private Issue**: Contact project maintainers for private discussion

Please include:
- Your contact information
- Description of the incident
- Witnesses (if applicable)
- Any supporting documentation

## Confidentiality

We are committed to maintaining confidentiality with respect to the reporter of an incident.

## Consequences

Anyone asked to stop unacceptable behavior is expected to comply immediately. If someone engages in unacceptable behavior, community leaders may take any action they deem appropriate, including a temporary or permanent ban from the community.

Actions may include:

1. **Warning**: A private warning with explanation of why behavior is not acceptable
2. **Temporary Suspension**: Temporary restriction from community participation
3. **Permanent Removal**: Permanent removal from the community

## Our Pledge

We pledge to act and enforce this code of conduct in a fair and consistent manner.

## FAQ

### What if I see a violation?

Please report it to the project maintainers as described in the Reporting Violations section. We take all reports seriously and will investigate promptly.

### What happens after I report?

1. You will receive acknowledgment within 24-48 hours
2. Investigation will begin
3. Appropriate action will be taken
4. You will be informed of the outcome (unless you prefer not to be)

### Can I remain anonymous?

Yes, you can report violations anonymously by emailing rutova2@gmail.com without providing your name or contact information.

### What if I disagree with an enforcement decision?

You can appeal enforcement decisions by contacting the project maintainers with your concern. Appeals will be reviewed by the team.

### What about freedom of speech?

This Code of Conduct is a set of community guidelines, not a legal document. We respect free speech within the bounds of these community guidelines. Private messages, discussions, and debate are encouraged, but must remain respectful.
